Guiding you to the web's best stories

WebSerialist.com is planned to be a one-stop shop for people looking for good web fiction. It will host links to stories on the web, allow users to rate and vote for stories, and it will be much easier to use than the other options out there. For more details on my plans for this site, see my blog post.
